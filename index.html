About Artisana Archives

Preserving contemporary art history through digital innovation

Our Mission

Artisana Archives is dedicated to preserving and showcasing contemporary art that explores the intersection of traditional craftsmanship and digital technology. We believe in creating accessible archives that bridge the gap between physical materiality and digital preservation.

Founded in 2015, our archive has grown to include works from over 200 artists across 35 countries, creating one of the most comprehensive collections of digitally-informed contemporary art.

Digital Preservation Initiative

Our Digital Preservation Initiative uses cutting-edge technology to create high-resolution 3D scans, virtual reality experiences, and blockchain-based authentication for contemporary artworks. This ensures that each piece in our collection is preserved for future generations while remaining accessible to global audiences.
